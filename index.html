Our Story

Born From a Passion for the Perfect Brew

BrewLab started in a small Nairobi kitchen with one obsession — finding coffee that actually tasted like it was supposed to. We traveled to farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Kenya, building relationships with growers who share our belief that quality starts at the root.

Today we roast every batch in-house, ship within 48 hours of roasting, and stand behind every bag we sell.
